For our final project, we wanted to analyze the calories attributed to coffee consumption and determine whether a linear regression is applicable. We used datasets from the National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey (NHANES) 2015-2016.

Specifically, we used

  • Demographics Data (DEMO_I.XPT)
  • Dietary Interview - Individual Foods, First Day (DR1IFF_I.XPT)
  • Dietary Interview Technical Support File - Food Codes (DRXFCD_I.XPT)

Variables in the final dataset include:

  • SEQN: Respondent sequence number
  • TOTAL: Total number of calories consumed
  • COFFEE_TOTAL: Total number of calories attributable to coffee consumption
  • GENDER: Gender
  • AGE: Age in years at screening
  • RACE: Race
    • Black
    • Hispanic
    • White
    • Other/Multi
  • EDUCATION: Education level
    • Less than high school
    • Some high school
    • High school graduate/GED
    • Some college/AA
    • College graduate or above
    • Unsure
  • MARITAL: Marital status
    • Married
    • Widowed
    • Divorced
    • Separated
    • Never married
    • Living with partner
    • Refused
    • Unsure
  • HH_SIZE: Total number of people in the household
  • HH_INC: Total household income
